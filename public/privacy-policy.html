Privacy Policy

Veritas — Catholic Apologetics

Effective date: April 8, 2026  •  Last updated: April 8, 2026

Rumblehold ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Veritas mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use Veritas on iOS, Android, or the web. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information stored locally on your device:

  • Your study progress, experience points (Halos), daily streaks, and mastered cards — stored entirely on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Your preference for the ad-free "Remove Ads" purchase.

Information collected by third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — If you use the free version of the App, Google AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the IDFA on iOS or GAID on Android), IP address, and information about your interaction with ads to serve and measure advertising. AdMob's content is filtered to G-rated categories. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — If you purchase "Remove Ads," RevenueCat processes your in-app purchase transaction. RevenueCat may collect device identifiers and purchase metadata to verify and manage your subscription or one-time purchase. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

We do not collect your name, email address, or any account credentials. The App requires no registration.

2. How We Use Information

We use information as follows:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the App's features.
  • To display advertising to free users (via Google AdMob).
  • To verify and fulfill in-app purchases (via RevenueCat).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Advertising

The free version of Veritas displays banner and interstitial ads served by Google AdMob. All ads are filtered to G-rated content, and categories including dating, gambling, alcohol, and adult content are blocked. AdMob may use device identifiers to personalize ads based on your interests. On iOS, we request permission before accessing the IDFA (App Tracking Transparency).

Purchasing "Remove Ads" ($4.99, one-time) disables all advertising permanently on your device.

4. In-App Purchases

The "Remove Ads" purchase is processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We use RevenueCat to verify and manage purchase entitlements. Apple and Google handle payment processing and do not share full payment details with us.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

All local study data (progress, streaks, XP) is stored on your device and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the App. We do not maintain server-side user records.

Data held by third-party services (AdMob, RevenueCat) is governed by their respective privacy policies and retention schedules.

6. Children's Privacy

Veritas is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data held about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of targeted advertising (via your device's ad settings or by purchasing "Remove Ads").
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

On iOS, you can limit ad tracking via Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. On Android, you can opt out of personalized ads via Settings → Google → Ads.

8. Third-Party Links

The App may display references to external websites or resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any third-party sites. We encourage you to review their policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
